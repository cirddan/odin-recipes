Pizza

Pizza is a savory dish that typically consists of a round or square shaped base made from dough and baked in an oven. The dough is usually topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. Pizza can be served as a whole pie or sliced into individual portions. It is a popular dish worldwide and has many variations and styles, including thin crust, deep dish, Neapolitan, and more.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp. dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to the desired thickness.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the tomato sauce, oregano, and garlic powder.
  4. Spread the sauce over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the sliced pepperoni on top of the cheese.
  7. Brush the edges of the pizza dough with olive oil.
  8. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
